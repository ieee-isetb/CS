About the Chapter

The IEEE ISET Bizerte Computer Society Chapter is the main source of information, inspiration and collaboration in our institute's IT community. Founded on December 28, 2020, the chapter has grown to 80 members and stands out for its dynamism and commitment to promoting computing technologies among students. Through flagship events such as hackathons, workshops, and training sessions, the CS chapter aims to develop technical skills, creativity, and team spirit while supporting members' personal and professional growth within the IEEE community.
